SHAP Summary Plot (shap_summary.png):
This plot shows the impact of each feature on the model's predictions across all test samples.
Features are ranked by importance (top to bottom). Red indicates a positive impact on the prediction, blue indicates a negative impact.

SHAP Force Plot (shap_force.png):
This plot shows the contribution of each feature to the prediction for the first test sample.
Features pushing the prediction higher are in red, and those pushing it lower are in blue.
